Benefits

Reasons why you should improve your typing speed

Time

Factor in time to think about what you want to say and the time it takes to start and stop if you need to look at the keyboard and you could be looking at close to an hour to get that task done.

Health

If you spend a significant amount of time at a desk, in front of a computer then you probably know that sitting and typing can take a toll on your body.

Focus

Think about every time you have to look at the keyboard to find the right key. Or, maybe you find yourself constantly making mistakes and having to go back and make corrections as you type.

Job Prospects

Typing is not an optional skill anymore. Many employers require computer skills and a certain typing speed to even be considered for some positions.
